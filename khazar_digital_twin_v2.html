Technical Background
1
Lake-basin water balance framing
Sea-level stress is interpreted from endorheic-basin controls: river inflow, evaporation forcing, precipitation, wind setup and shoreline hypsometry.
2
Remote-sensing evidence separation
Optical, thermal, altimetry, bathymetry and coastline layers remain separate until the export/audit stage, reducing hidden model mixing.
3
Calibration overlay model
Uploaded observations are validated against a fixed metric schema and added as an overlay, preserving the original data layout and baseline interpretation.
4
Scenario delta comparison
Compare mode reports deltas in water level, surface area, inflow health, alert severity and confidence from the same live state.
